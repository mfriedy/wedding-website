Flights

Daniel K. Inouye International Airport is the airport on Oahu in Honolulu. There are direct flights from Sydney and connecting flights from the Midwest.

We will be flying in on Qantas flight 103 on Sunday 5th October, arriving at 9:00AM. We will be departing on Qantas flight 104 on Saturday 11th October, leaving at 11:00AM. Once you have your flights booked, please let us know your arrival/departures.

If you decide to extend your holiday, there are inter-island flights from the Honolulu airport. Southwest and Hawaiian airlines flies to each island. If you decide to visit Kauai or the Big Island, ask us for recommendations!

Hotels

The wedding ceremony and reception will be held at Halekulani Hotel in Waikiki beach. We will be staying here the 9th of October and will be staying at the Embassay suites by Hilton on Waikiki beach the other nights.

Waikiki Beach has a plethora of hotels right on the beach or just in from the beach. There are chain hotels or small hotels with kitchens available. Please book what best suites your needs and ask us if you need any assistance.

Airbnb's are heavily regulated on Oahu. They are limited to very specific areas on the island. If you decide to stay in an airbnb please ensure the booking information includes the government rental code, meaning it is approved for short term rentals.

Transport

Uber is used on Oahu and is easily available. It is about a 20-30 minute uber from Honolulu airport to Waikiki beach.

Rental cars are also available for hire on Oahu if you would like to explore other areas of the island. Please note parking in Waikiki beach could prove expensive and difficult.

Transport will not be provided to or from the wedding venue.
